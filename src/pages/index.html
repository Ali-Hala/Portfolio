About Me

I am a mechatronics engineering student at the University of Waterloo, passionate about designing and building innovative solutions that integrate mechanical, electrical, and software systems. My academic journey, along with my hands-on experience from co-op placements, has equipped me with strong foundations in mechanical design, numerical methods, computer vision, and real-time system programming. I thrive in dynamic, collaborative environments where I can apply my problem-solving skills to real-world challenges. With a keen interest in automation and robotics, I am driven to create technologies that improve efficiency, sustainability, and user experience. Outside of academics, I enjoy exploring new tech trends, tinkering with personal projects, and engaging in outdoor activities to stay active and inspired.
